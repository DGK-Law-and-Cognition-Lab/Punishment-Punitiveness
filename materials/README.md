# Materials Directory

## Survey Instrument

**`stimuli/PUNISHMENT_21_-__Pro-Social.docx`**
- Complete survey instrument with all scales and items
- Three vignette conditions (second-degree murder scenarios)
- Response format specifications

### Vignette Conditions
1. **Stranger felony-murder** — robbery resulting in death
2. **Domestic violence** — intimate partner dispute resulting in death
3. **Organized crime** — road rage altercation resulting in death

All vignettes present second-degree murder with sentencing guidelines of 20–30 years (range 10–life).
