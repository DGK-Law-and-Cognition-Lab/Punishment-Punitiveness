## Vignette Content

### Vignette 1: Domestic Dispute
Second-degree murder arising from a domestic argument. Perpetrator and victim were in an intimate relationship.

### Vignette 2: Robbery
Second-degree murder during a robbery. Perpetrator and victim were strangers.

### Vignette 3: Road Rage
Second-degree murder arising from a traffic altercation. Perpetrator and victim were strangers.

All vignettes provide:
- Basic case facts
- Judicial sentencing guidelines (20-30 years typical, range 10-life)
- Uniform format for comparison
